Top 6-10!

6. 18 Again

Staring: Ha-neul Kim, Yoon Sang-Hyun, Lee Do-Hyun
Genre: Comedy, Fantasy, Romance

A 37-year-old man on the verge of being divorced from his wife suddenly finds himself inside his 18-year-old body. He starts living a new life under a new name to get closer to his children and protect them.

7. Healer

Staring: Ji Chang-Wook, Park Min-Young, Tae-Mi
Genre: Action, Romance, Crime

An old murder incident involving a group who ran an illegal broadcasting station brings together different people - a mysterious errand guy "Healer" who possesses disguise and fighting skills, a reporter from a second-rate tabloid, and a famous journalist. They will have to deal with the cruel conflict of truth versus faith.

8. Scarlet Heart Ryeo

Staring: Lee Joon-Gi, Ji eun-Lee, Kang Ha-Neul
Genre: Drama, Fantasy, History

Korean melodramatic adaptation of Chinese drama Scarlet Heart (2011) follows a woman who travels back in time to the Goryeo Dynasty of Korea whenever a total eclipse of the sun took place.

9. Mr Queen

Staring: Hye-Sun Shin, Kim Jung-Hyun, Seol In-ah
Genre: Crime, Fantasy, Romance

When a modern day chef gets trapped in the body of a queen in the Joseon era, chaos ensues.

10. While You were Sleeping

Staring: Bae Suzy, Lee Jong-Suk, Jung Hae-In
Genre: Comedy, Fantasy, Romance

The drama is about a woman, Nam Hong Joo, who can see accidents that take place in the future through her dreams. And a prosecutor, Jung Jae Chan, who struggles to stop the woman's dreams from coming true.
